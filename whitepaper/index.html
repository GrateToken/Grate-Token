Vision & Purpose

The mission of $GRATE is to establish a strong and sustainable ecosystem that evolves with the needs of its holders. Unlike short-lived meme tokens, $GRATE is designed for long-term growth with a focus on community engagement, token distribution incentives, and market stability.

Release & Distribution Schedule

Phase 1: Pre-Bonding (Current)

  • Deployment on Solana via Pump.fun
  • Initial community building on X & Telegram
  • Early buy-ins and organic growth
  • Liquidity pool expansion towards bonding

Phase 2: Bonding to Raydium (Upcoming)

  • Liquidity locked permanently to ensure price stability
  • Token burning of unbonded supply to reduce inflation
  • Community engagement initiatives to drive adoption
  • Giveaway structure launch to reward active holders
